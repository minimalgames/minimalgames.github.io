How to Play

Pulse is a timing game that trains your internal clock. The goal is simple: release at exactly the target time.

Zen Mode

Guided rhythm training with haptic feedback

  • Hold the button to start the timer
  • Feel the haptic pulses guiding you every second
  • Release at exactly the target time
  • Build streaks by staying within the target range
  • Adjust the target time using the slider (1s - 10s)

Chaos Mode

No rhythm. No haptics. Pure instinct.

  • Hold the button to start the timer
  • No haptic feedback - trust your internal clock
  • Random target times keep you on your toes
  • Release at the right moment using only your instinct
  • The ultimate test of your timing precision

Scoring System

  • Perfect: Within ±0.010s of target (1000 points)
  • Incredible: Within ±0.050s of target (975 points)
  • Excellent: Within ±0.100s of target (950 points)
  • Great: Within ±0.150s of target (900 points)
  • Good: Within ±0.200s of target (850 points)
  • Nice: Within ±0.300s of target (750 points)
  • Okay: Within ±0.500s of target (600 points)
  • Miss: Beyond ±0.500s (lower points)

Tips for Success

  • Start with Zen Mode: Use the haptic feedback to develop your internal rhythm
  • Focus on consistency: Build streaks by staying within range
  • Find your rhythm: Count in your head or feel the beat
  • Stay relaxed: Tension affects timing - breathe and stay calm
  • Practice regularly: Your internal clock improves with training
  • Challenge yourself: Once comfortable, try Chaos Mode

Common Questions

Why isn't haptic feedback working?

  • Check that your device supports haptics (most modern iPhones do)
  • Make sure your device isn't in silent mode or Do Not Disturb
  • Check your device's haptic settings in iOS Settings
  • Note: Haptics are only available in Zen Mode

How do I reset my statistics?

  • Currently, stats are stored locally on your device
  • To reset, you would need to reinstall the app
  • A reset option may be added in future updates

Can I change the target time in Chaos Mode?

  • No - Chaos Mode uses random target times to keep you challenged
  • This is by design to test your adaptability

Is my data stored online?

  • No - all data is stored locally on your device only
  • Your scores, streaks, and settings never leave your device
  • See our Privacy Policy for more details

Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems:

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Try force-closing and restarting the app
  • Restart your device if problems persist
  • Check that your iOS is up to date
