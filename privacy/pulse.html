Privacy Policy for Pulse

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Overview

Pulse ("the App") is a timing and rhythm training mobile application developed by Daniel Fry. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App:

  • Does not require an account, registration, or login
  • Does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any contact information
  • Does not track your location or device identifiers
  • Does not access your camera, microphone, photos, or contacts
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or telemetry services
  • Does not contain advertisements or ad tracking
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Data Storage

All game data (including scores, best times, streaks, statistics, and preferences) is stored exclusively on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server or cloud service
  • Is not backed up to any external service
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any other entity
  • Can be permanently deleted by uninstalling the App

Haptic Feedback

The App uses haptic feedback (device vibration) to enhance the timing experience. This is a local device function and does not involve any data collection or transmission.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with, connect to, or use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that could collect user data.

Internet Connection

The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not transmit any data over the internet.

Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our App.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the App
  • No data recovery is possible after uninstallation as we do not store backups

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. If we make material changes to how we handle user data, we will notify users through the App or app store listing.
