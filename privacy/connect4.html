Privacy Policy for Four in a Row

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

Overview

Four in a Row ("the App") is a mobile game developed by Daniel Fry. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The App:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your camera, microphone, or photos
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertisements

Data Storage

All game data (scores, statistics, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
