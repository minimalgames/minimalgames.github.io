Need help with one of our games? We're here to assist you.

Four in a Row

How to Play:

  • Tap a column to drop your disc
  • Connect four discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) to win
  • Take turns with your opponent
  • The game ends when someone wins or the board is full

Common Questions:

  • How do I reset my stats? Go to the scoreboard and tap the reset button
  • Can I play online? Currently, Four in a Row supports local two-player mode only
  • How do I undo a move? Moves cannot be undone - this is part of the classic gameplay
